About Us

MountainMade Property Care provides reliable, affordable yard and property maintenance for homeowners who want fast, dependable results. Founded by two college students, we focus on practical services like debris cleanup, lawn trimming, gutter clearing, firewood handling, and light haul-offs. Our goal is to make your property look clean, organized, and well-kept without the hassle.

Services & Pricing Guide

We base our pricing on a few simple factors: yard size, time required, debris amount, and overall labor difficulty. This helps us give every customer a fair and accurate quote.

Most jobs fall between $60–$120, depending on what’s needed. You’ll always get a clear estimate before we start.
